A romance trope is a commonly used plot device or theme that appears frequently in romantic fiction.

LOVE TRIANGLE

In which two characters compete for the affection of a third.

FORBIDDEN LOVE

The main characters must navigate societal disapproval and discrimination to be together.

THE CONFUSED WOMAN

In which a heterosexual woman discovers her attraction to other women and must come to terms with her sexuality.

OPPOSITES ATTRACT

Two characters who have very different personalities or backgrounds are drawn to each other.

RESCUE

Involves one character rescuing or saving the other character from a dangerous or challenging situation, either physically or emotionally.

SECOND CHANCE

The main characters have a history together and meet again later, giving them a second chance at love.

ROOMMATES

Involves two characters who are forced to live together in close quarters.

BUTCH/FEMME

A dynamic in which one character is more traditionally masculine or dominant, while the other is more feminine or submissive.
